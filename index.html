07 — FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you respond to a security incident?

Our AI-powered SOC detects and responds to threats in under 1.2 seconds on average. For human escalation, our 24/7 team has a 15-minute SLA for critical incidents and dedicated incident response specialists available within 30 minutes.

What compliance frameworks do you support?

We support SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, HIPAA, PCI DSS, GDPR, FedRAMP, FISMA, NIST 800-171, CMMC, and more. Our platform automates compliance evidence collection and includes pre-built policy templates for each framework.

Can you integrate with our existing security tools?

Yes. We integrate with 100+ security tools including SIEMs, EDR platforms, cloud providers (AWS, Azure, GCP), CI/CD pipelines, and identity providers. Our open API allows custom integrations when needed.

What makes your AI security different?

Our AI models are trained on 10B+ threat indicators and continuously learn from every incident across our customer base. We achieve 99.7% detection accuracy with 0.01% false positive rate, significantly better than traditional signature-based systems.

Do you offer month-to-month contracts?

Yes. While we offer discounts for annual commitments, all plans are available on a month-to-month basis with no long-term contracts required. You can cancel or adjust your plan at any time.

How do you handle data privacy?

All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). We're SOC 2 Type II certified, GDPR compliant, and undergo annual third-party security audits. Customer data is never used for AI training without explicit consent and anonymization.
